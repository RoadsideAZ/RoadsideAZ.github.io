Services

What we offer

Battery Replacement

Roadside A-Z provides professional battery replacement services. Wherever you are, we come to you and replace your battery!

Oil Change

Having difficulty scheduling an oil change with your service station? That's no issue for Roadside A-Z as our technicians have the experience and know-how to change your cars oil right at home or wherever your car is located!

Tire Change

Getting stuck with a flat tire is annoying and that's why our goal is to get you back on the road as soon as possible. Our Technicians will come out and either plug the flat tire or just swap it out for the spare tire to get your vehicle moving again!

Lockouts

Locked yourself out of your car? That's no issue for Roadside A-Z Technicians. We come prepared with the right tools to get your car unlocked!

Battery Diagnoses

Roadside A-Z Technicians have the proper diagnostic tools to test your battery health! We will let you know if your battery needs to be recharged, replaced soon, or replaced now so you can make the best decision for your vehicle!

Jump Starts

If all you need is a quick jump start to get your car going, Roadside A-Z Technicians come equipped with powerful tools to get most cars back on the road within minutes!

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I schedule services?

    Scheduling services is easy! Simply call us at one of our phone numbers or email us here!

  • What type of cars can you service?

    Our technicians are equipped with tools to service most vehicles types including hybrid and electric cars. We do not provide services to box trucks, RV, or bigger vehicles.

  • Do I need to have a store-bought car battery for installation?

    No. Roadside A-Z Technicians come with batteries in their vehicles. We provide you batteries at competitive prices and also install the battery for free!

  • What areas do you service?

    Roadside A-Z mainly provides service to Queens and Long Island in New York, however we are willing to service outside areas depending on the service requested. Please contact us below if you need a specific answer on a specific area.
